# Module 4: The OpenClaw Dashboard & Cron Jobs

### 4.1 Accessing the OpenClaw Dashboard
After linking your Telegram ID, OpenClaw will ask if you want to open the dashboard.
- Opt to launch the GUI (Graphical User Interface) Dashboard. This allows you to visually overview configurations and tasks seamlessly.

**Note:** Though the dashboard is visual, your Telegram bot channel allows you to orchestrate the Assistant via messaging!

### 4.2 Setting Up Your First Cron Job
A cron job is simply a scheduled background task that triggers at a stated time.

To test out a background schedule:
1. Open the Telegram chat with your bot.
2. Send a regular, natural-language text.
   - Example Input: `"Remind me at 3 PM to drink water"`
3. OpenClaw will autonomously interpret your command into a cron job and apply the requested schedule natively.
